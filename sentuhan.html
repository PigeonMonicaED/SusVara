Trade Your Clothes, Get a Discount!

If, for any reason, you feel it's time to part ways with your SusVara piece or your old clothes, please don’t throw them away. Exchange them with us through our Circularity Campaign to help prevent them from ending up in landfills, which contribute to waste buildup. Instead, we will recycle the fabrics into high-quality, eco-friendly yarn that will be used to create our Earth’s Touch Bundle Collection.

By trading in three of your old clothes, you will receive a 30% discount voucher applicable to all of our products.

About Me

SusVara mendukung gerakan slow fashion dengan menolak fast fashion berlebihan. Kami fokus pada desain abadi, bahan berkualitas, dan produksi yang etis serta ramah lingkungan. Kami menginspirasi perubahan menuju mode yang lebih sadar dan berkelanjutan.
