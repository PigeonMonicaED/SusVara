SusVara comes from the combination of sustainability and svara. Sustainability reflects our commitment to sustainable fashion, prioritizing quality, ethics, and positive impact.

Svara, derived from Sanskrit, means voice and
symbolizes expression and transformation in the fashion industry.

About Me

SusVara mendukung gerakan slow fashion dengan menolak fast fashion berlebihan. Kami fokus pada desain abadi, bahan berkualitas, dan produksi yang etis serta ramah lingkungan. Kami menginspirasi perubahan menuju mode yang lebih sadar dan berkelanjutan.
