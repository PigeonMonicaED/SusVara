Your SusVara pieces are meticulously crafted by hand, with patience and intention. Just like any meaningful relationship in your life, they deserve thoughtful care and attention.
Handwashing is not only gentler on the Earth but also helps preserve the richness of natural dyes, ensuring your pieces remain as vibrant as the day you received them. More than that, it transforms a simple task into a ritual of appreciation—deepening your connection with what you own and the artisans who brought it to life.

Caring is Radical

Life is unpredictable, no matter how well you plan. Buttons come loose, fabric catches on something, little accidents happen. But before replacing, always consider repairing—it’s a small choice that makes a big difference.

Every SusVara piece is designed to last, and we stand by that with our promise of lifetime repair. Along with our dedicated repair service, we also support your own DIY fixes with our repair guide, repair kits, and those spare buttons you might need. Because caring for what you have is the most sustainable choice of all.

Lifetime Repair Guarantee

Despite your best efforts to care for them, there comes a time when you have to say goodbye to your favorite clothes. When that happens to your SusVara piece, don’t throw it away—send it back to us. We’re in the process of setting up a recycling program to give your old clothes a second life. And if they’ve truly reached the end of their journey, we’ll compost them for you, returning them to the Earth as gently as they were made.

Like everything we do, it’s about progress, not perfection. Together, let’s close the loop and make fashion truly sustainable.

About Me
